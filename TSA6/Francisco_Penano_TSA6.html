Luffy (モンキー・D・ルフィ)

4th Emperor of The Sea

Monkey D. Luffy is the captain of the Straw Hat Pirates, and dreamt of being a pirate since childhood from the influence of his idol and mentor Red-Haired Shanks

Buggy (バギー)

Devil Fruit User

Also known as Buggy the Clown (道化のバギー, Dōke no Bagī), is the captain of the Buggy Pirates as well as the co-leader of the Buggy Pirate Alliance, and a former apprentice of the Roger Pirates.

Desert King "Sir Crocodile"

Devil Fruit User

Kaido

3rd Emperor of the Sea

Also known as the King of the Beasts, is a major antagonist in the One Piece franchise. He is the Governor-General of the Beasts Pirates, a former member of the Four Emperors and was a member of the legendary Rocks Pirates as an apprentice forty years ago alongside prominent members like Big Mom and Whitebeard.

BigMom

2nd Emperor of the Sea

Charlotte Linlin, better known as "Big Mom", is the captain of the Big Mom Pirates and was formerly one of the Four Emperors ruling over the New World, as the only female member of that group. She is the matriarch of the very large Charlotte Family, whose many children make up the infrastructure of her crew.
