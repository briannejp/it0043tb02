Kakashi Hatake

Kakashi Hatake ( はたけ カカシ) In Naruto, Kakashi is shown as a peculiar yet extremely talented shinobi for the Hidden Leaf Village. Because of his father Sakumo's suicide, Kakashi was cold and calculated at first.

Itachi Uchiha

Itachi Uchiha (うちは イタチ, Uchiha Itachi) is a character that Masashi Kishimoto created for the manga and anime series Naruto. Sasuke Uchiha's elder brother Itachi is to blame for the deaths of all the senior members of his clan, leaving only Sasuke alive.

Hinata Hyuga

Hinata Hyuga (日向 ヒナタ, Hyūga Hinata) The fact that Hinata consistently addresses everybody with appropriate honorifics, including the captains of the Gotei 13, speaks volumes about her qualities as a quiet, contemplative, calm, kind, and extremely polite person.
