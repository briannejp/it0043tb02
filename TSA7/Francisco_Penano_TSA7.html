Naruto Uzumaki

Naruto Uzumaki (Japanese: うずまき ナルト) He is the loudest ninja in Hidden Leaf, hands down. He is not as talented as his fellow Academy students, he disobeys the rules, he needs to be the center of attention, and his IQ is inferior to that of every other ninja.

Sasuke Uchiha

Sasuke Uchiha (Japanese: うちは サスケ) As a child, Sasuke was kind and cheerful, but that all changed when he was witnessed the massacre of his clan at age 7. Now, Sasuke's only goal in life is revenge, and he'll do whatever he needs to get it.

Sakura Haruno

Sakura Haruno (Japanese: 春野 サクラ) At her core, Sakura is brave, empathetic, and selfless; she would fight against someone she knows are better than her in order to protect her squad.

Kakashi Hatake

Kakashi Hatake ( はたけ カカシ) In Naruto, Kakashi is shown as a peculiar yet extremely talented shinobi for the Hidden Leaf Village. Because of his father Sakumo's suicide, Kakashi was cold and calculated at first.

Itachi Uchiha

Itachi Uchiha (うちは イタチ, Uchiha Itachi) is a character that Masashi Kishimoto created for the manga and anime series Naruto. Sasuke Uchiha's elder brother Itachi is to blame for the deaths of all the senior members of his clan, leaving only Sasuke alive.

Hinata Hyuga

Hinata Hyuga (日向 ヒナタ, Hyūga Hinata) The fact that Hinata consistently addresses everybody with appropriate honorifics, including the captains of the Gotei 13, speaks volumes about her qualities as a quiet, contemplative, calm, kind, and extremely polite person.

Naruto (2024 Remake)

Naruto (NARUTO -ナルト-, Naruto) is the the remake of the 2002 anime and the second anime adaptation developed by Bones based on the Part l of Naruto manga by Masashi Kishimoto and is directed by Keitaro (me) and written by Hiroshi Ōnogi. The series premiered on May 10, 2014, on Animax Sunday 5:00 pm. The series has some differences with the manga and the original anime. In the original anime, the others characters like Shikamaru, Kiba, Ino and etc. appears in First Episode. While in 2024 anime, they just appears on Episode 21, like the manga that these characters appears in Chunin Exams Arc. In the manga/original anime, in the fight against Zabuza, Sasuke and Naruto are the ones who plan to attack him with the Fuma Shuriken. in the 2024 anime, Sakura who are came up with the Fuma Shuriken strategy. In the manga/original anime, Naruto is swallowed by a snake and escapes from it making the Multi-Shadow Clones. While the 2024 version, this scene does not happen, then Naruto is not swallowed by a snake in this anime. Some characters received some changes in personality, such as Sakura, Hinata and Naruto (which was the least modified).
